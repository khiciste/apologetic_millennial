AUTHORITY / SUBVERSION

Related to our ability to determine who’s “in charge,” and form beneficial relationships up and down hierarchies. Helps us create and maintain orderly societies.

CLOSE

LOYALTY / BETRAYAL

Related to our history as tribal creatures, able to form coalitions that provide belonging and protection. A loyal tribe is a living tribe.

CLOSE

FAIRNESS / CHEATING

Related to “The Golden Rule” and our desire to create justice in an unfair world. “You scratch my back, I’ll scratch yours—or else.”

CLOSE

CARE / HARM

Related to our ability to form attachments with our offspring and help ensure their survival. It helps us to feel, dislike, and seek to minimize, the pain of others.

CLOSE

SANCTITY / DEGRADATION

Related to our tendency to be disgusted by things like excrement or rotting meat. Helps us avoid disease and infection. Progressed to include purity of mind as well as body.

Note: sanctity = purity

FREEDOM / OPPRESSION

Related to our feelings of resentment of those who try to coerce, control, or dominate us in any way. Don’t tread on me.
