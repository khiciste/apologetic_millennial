USING PSYCHOLOGY TO HIGHLIGHT THE WAYS WE TALK RIGHT PAST EACH OTHER.

WHY ME — WHY NOW

If you’ve ever written someone off because of their politics, or you’re tired of political conversations that end up exactly where they started, you’ve come to the right place.

I've spent the last 15 years down various rabbit holes about psychology and political polarization and the last four years weaving those experiences into a book. For now, I offer snippets of this ongoing journey to have a political conversation that doesn’t end with someone (me) shouting and/or crying.
